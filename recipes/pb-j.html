PB&J

Description

The perfect lazy snack, all this sandwich needs is three ingredients - 2 of them are in the name!

Ingredients

  • Bread (I recommend Italian)
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly (I recommend grape)

Steps

  1. Spread jelly on one slice
  2. Spread peanut butter on the other
  3. Put it together and slice through the middle if you like!
